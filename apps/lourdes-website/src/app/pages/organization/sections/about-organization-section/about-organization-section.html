Our Organization

The parish structure hasn't changed significantly with the federation. It's more of a shift in responsibilities. The parish council has delegated a number of tasks to the federation level. The pastoral group has been given more responsibility and has become the pastoral heart of the parish.

The need for cooperation is dictated by a shortage of priests and the declining number of volunteers. In the new organisation there is a personal union and a federation. By joining forces in certain areas, it remains possible to serve five parishes and keep them independent.
