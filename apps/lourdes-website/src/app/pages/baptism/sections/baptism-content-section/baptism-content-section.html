A New Beginning - The Sacrament of Baptism

Baptism is a special and sacred moment. A sign of new life, faith, and connection with God. Through baptism, the Church welcomes you or your child into the community of believers—a moment filled with meaning, joy, and blessing.

Would you like to be baptized, or have your child baptized? This form makes it easy to submit a baptism request. Once we receive it, we will contact you to discuss the preparations.

Please complete the form in full so we can process your request properly and carefully prepare this special moment.

Important Information

Preparatory Meeting: The baptism service takes place on the second Saturday of the month. Prior to the baptism, there will be a preparatory meeting.

This preparatory meeting will be held in a baby-area manner at one central location (in de Emmauspargers, Reyerdijk 67, 3079 KK Rotterdam). The meeting will be from 8:00 PM to approximately 10:00 PM. The baptism will take place in the parish church of your choice.

Please note that the mandatory preparatory meeting may take place 4 to 5 weeks before the baptism date. (See the baptism date selection for this date).

Photo Requirement: In addition to the parent(s), the godparent(s) should also be present at the preparatory meeting. This evening is for informational purposes only. Please do not bring your child if possible. Please bring a photo of your child.

Parish Fee: The (usual) fee for a baptism service is €75. This must be paid in advance or at the preparatory evening.

Parish Contribution: The parish relies entirely on collections, donations, and contributions from parishioners. This contribution helps keep the parish running.

Please submit this form in a timely manner. We will contact you to make further arrangements.
