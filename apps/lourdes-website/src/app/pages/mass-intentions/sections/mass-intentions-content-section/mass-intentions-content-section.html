Important Information

One intention per form

We request that you submit only one intention via this form. You can, however, indicate that you would like to repeat this intention (several times).

Paying the stipend

We request that you pay the stipend upon submitting this form to the parish office, or by transferring it to ING account NL70 INGB 0001779874, payable to H. Ehn-Leitheid Rotterdam, with the reference "Mass intention for ... (date)".

Administration time

If you transfer the amount to our bank account, please make a copy of the payment order and include it with this form. If the Mass intention and proof of payment are received by Thursday at the latest, your Mass intention can be collected for the following Sunday.

GDPR Statement
