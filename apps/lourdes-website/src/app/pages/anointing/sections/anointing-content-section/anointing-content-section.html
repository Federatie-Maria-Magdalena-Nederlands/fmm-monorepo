Strength, Comfort and Peace – The Sacrament of the Anointing of the Sick

The Anointing of the Sick is a sign of God's closeness, love, and comfort in times of illness or vulnerability. It is a sacred moment in which the priest prays for strength, healing, and inner peace, and the sick person is anointed with consecrated oil.

This sacrament brings hope and peace—not only to the sick person, but also to those around them. It reminds us that we are never alone: God is with us, especially in moments of sorrow and pain.
