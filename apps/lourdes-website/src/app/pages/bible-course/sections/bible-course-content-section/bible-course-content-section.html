Discover the Word of God

Are you curious about the Bible but not sure where to begin? Or do you read regularly but would like to understand it better and discuss it together? Then we warmly invite you to our Bible study at the Lourdes Parish!

During this course, we'll embark on a step-by-step journey of discovery through the Bible. We'll take the time to better understand texts, see connections, ask questions, share insights, and discover what God's Word means for our lives today. Whether you're already well-versed or just starting out—everyone is welcome.

In a warm, open and pleasant atmosphere we learn together more about: The background and meaning of Bible stories How the Bible can provide direction in daily life Deepening faith through conversation and prayer
