A Life of Service to God – The Sacrament of Ordination

Ordination is a special vocation: a choice to dedicate one's life entirely to God and the community. Through the sacrament of Holy Orders, one is called to become a priest, deacon, or bishop—a sign of dedication, self-giving, and love for the Church.

Those who feel called to this path are listening to a deep inner longing to serve, lead, and share in Christ's mission. It is a path of faith, prayer, and formation—a lifelong journey with God at your side.
