Make a Difference Through Service

Volunteering in our parish is a wonderful way to share your time, talents, and gifts while building meaningful connections with fellow parishioners. Whether you have a few hours a week or can commit to a regular ministry, there's a place for you to serve.

Our volunteers are the heartbeat of our parish community. Through their dedicated service, they help create a welcoming environment, support worship celebrations, care for those in need, and ensure our parish runs smoothly. Every contribution, no matter how small, makes a significant impact.

Why Volunteer?

  • Live out your faith through practical service
  • Build meaningful relationships with fellow parishioners
  • Develop new skills and share your existing talents
  • Make a positive impact in your community
  • Experience the joy that comes from serving others

We have various volunteer opportunities available across different ministries and services. Whether you're interested in liturgical ministries, administrative support, pastoral care, or facility maintenance, we'll help you find the perfect fit for your interests and availability.

Complete the form below to express your interest in volunteering. Let us know which areas you're passionate about, and we'll be in touch to discuss how you can get involved.
