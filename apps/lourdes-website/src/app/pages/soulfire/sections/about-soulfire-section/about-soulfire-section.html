Young people are the future of our Church.

At the Catholic Youth Group Soulfire, they are given the space to deepen their faith, connect with one another, and grow together. What began in the Lourdes Parish now lives on in the Emmaus Parish on Reyerdijk – and young people from all over Rotterdam are warmly welcome!

The group is led by Father Cândido , who guides young people on their faith journey with enthusiasm and warmth. Together they celebrate Mass, have inspiring conversations, organize outings, volunteer, and, above all, experience a great deal of joy and connection.
