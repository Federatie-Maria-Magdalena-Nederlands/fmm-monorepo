A place of prayer, meeting and faith

In the Lourdes parish on the Slinge in Rotterdam, a warm and diverse community gathers to celebrate their faith. Our services are moments of peace, reflection, and connection—with God and with each other. Whether you've been involved for years or are visiting for the first time, you are warmly welcome just as you are.

Every celebration contributes to keeping faith alive in our daily lives. With inspiring words, moving music, and connecting rituals, we aim to create space for faith, hope, and love. We believe that God still speaks, works, and brings people together today – right here, in the heart of South Rotterdam.

Be encouraged, be nourished, and be welcomed. For together we are the living church.
