Join Our Parish Family

Are you looking for a place where your faith can grow, where you can meet people with an open heart, and where you are truly seen? Then you are very welcome at the Lourdes Parish on the Slinge in Rotterdam!

Our parish is a colorful, engaged, and welcoming community where young and old come together to celebrate, pray, and build a better world. Whether you want to come to church every Sunday, have your children baptized, actively help out, or simply seek some peace and quiet now and then, there's always a place for you.

Benefits of Membership

  • You belong to a close-knit religious community
  • You will receive information about celebrations, activities and sacraments
  • You can actively contribute with your talents and time
  • You help build a vibrant church in the neighborhood

The registration process is simple and straightforward. Please complete the form below with your information. Once registered, you'll receive a confirmation and welcome package with more information about parish life and how to get involved.
