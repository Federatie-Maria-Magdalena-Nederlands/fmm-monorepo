A Place of Faith and Warmth

The Lourdes Parish is more than just a building. It's a vibrant faith community where people gather to pray, celebrate, support, and grow. A place of encounter, hope, and comfort—right in the heart of South Rotterdam.

But to maintain this house of faith and hospitality, we need your help.

Every day we dedicate ourselves to liturgy, pastoral care, children's activities, elderly visits, catechesis, church building maintenance, and organizing beautiful services. Much of this is accomplished through the efforts of volunteers, but also with fixed costs for heating, lighting, maintenance, flowers, liturgical materials, and support for those in need.

Your donation is necessary to:

  • • Keeping the doors of the church open for everyone
  • • To enable activities for young and old
  • • To maintain and heat the church building
  • • Being close to people in difficult times

Your gift—small or large—contributes directly to the future of our parish.

Together, we bear responsibility for this house of faith.
